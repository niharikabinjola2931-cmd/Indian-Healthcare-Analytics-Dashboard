# 🏥 Indian Healthcare Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Indian Healthcare Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project developed to analyze healthcare data and provide meaningful insights into patient demographics, treatment costs, hospital performance, and treatment patterns.

The dashboard enables users to explore healthcare data through interactive filters, KPIs, and visualizations, supporting data-driven decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Objective

The primary objectives of this project are:

- Analyze patient distribution across different regions.
- Compare treatment costs among different hospital types.
- Study the relationship between income level and death rates.
- Analyze patient demographics and blood group distribution.
- Identify treatment patterns and hospital performance.
- Create an interactive dashboard for healthcare analysis.

# 📈 Key Insights

- Male patients accounted for **49.63%** of total admissions.
- Private hospitals recorded the highest average treatment cost (**₹237.30K**), while Community Health Centers had the lowest (**₹31.68K**).
- **B+** was the most common blood group among patients, whereas **AB−** was the least common.
- The North region recorded the highest patient count, while the Northeast had the lowest.
- Lower-income patients represented the highest number of admissions and recorded the highest death count.
- Uttar Pradesh had the highest patient count among all states.
- Medication was the most frequently used treatment type, while Palliative Care was the least used.
